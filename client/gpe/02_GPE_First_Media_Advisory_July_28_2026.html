Atlanta Communities Come Together to Defend Environmental Justice + Climate Resilience

Residents, advocates, elected officials, and environmental leaders will gather to highlight community-led solutions and call for continued protections as federal rollbacks threaten progress on environmental justice.

Why This Matters

Across Atlanta, communities face rising temperatures, poor air quality, increasing energy burdens, and disproportionate exposure to environmental hazards. Speakers will discuss how federal rollbacks to environmental justice programs and environmental protections could affect frontline communities while highlighting the local leadership and investments needed to build healthier, safer, and more resilient neighborhoods.

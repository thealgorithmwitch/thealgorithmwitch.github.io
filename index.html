Common Questions

The Scroll of Answers

Do you work with organizations outside of climate and energy?
Yes — my primary field is climate, clean energy, and policy, but my methodology applies to any mission-driven organization working in public education, advocacy, or community organizing. If your work requires building collective power through digital channels, we're likely aligned.
What does a typical engagement look like?
Most projects begin with a discovery call followed by a scoping document. Retainer engagements run monthly with defined deliverables. Project-based work (Website Revamp, Brand Kit, Metrics Intensive) follows a defined process with clear milestones and a final delivery date.
Can I hire you for just a single project?
Absolutely. The Website Revamp, Brand Kit Revamp, and Digital Metrics Intensive are all available as standalone projects. Each has a defined scope, timeline, and fixed starting price. Reach out with context on your organization and I'll let you know if we're a fit.
How far out are you currently booking?
Availability shifts with project load. The best way to find out is to reach out directly. I take on a limited number of clients at a time to ensure quality and depth of engagement. If timing isn't right, I'll tell you honestly and we can plan for a future window.
Do you offer sliding scale pricing for small nonprofits?
On a case-by-case basis, yes. I'm committed to the mission-driven sector and understand that budget constraints are real. If your organization is early-stage or under-resourced, mention that in your message and we'll see what's possible.

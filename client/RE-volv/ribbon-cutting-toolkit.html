Welcome

Celebrate the moment your energy savings begin.

We are thrilled to help you celebrate the completion of your solar installation and the start of your energy savings. This toolkit contains resources to help you prepare for and promote your solar celebration.

All of these tools are completely optional and meant to function as a guide. Please select the resources that best fit your organization and staff capacity.

Communications Support

RE-volv Coordination & Media Support

To help maximize your event’s reach, please integrate RE-volv into your workflow at each key stage.

Press Release Review

Before distributing any press release, please send it to RE-volv at least 72 hours in advance so our team can review it, suggest edits if needed, and provide quotes or additional information.

Social Media Collaboration

Tag RE-volv on all social media posts before, during, and after your event so we can amplify your celebration across our channels.

Post-Event Photos & Video

After your event, please share:

  • High-resolution event photos
  • Ribbon cutting photos
  • Community photos
  • Short-form vertical videos
  • Landscape video, if available

These materials help us celebrate your success, report impact to supporters, and inspire future nonprofit solar projects.

Ribbon Cutting Overview

A visible milestone for clean energy and community impact.

A ribbon cutting is a public celebration of your transition to clean solar energy. It marks the moment your organization begins generating its own power, reducing its carbon footprint, and reinvesting energy savings back into the community.

Broaden Your Mission’s Reach

Introduce new members, local officials, media outlets, civic leaders, and community partners to your mission and long-term goals.

Ripple Solar Through Your Community

One visible nonprofit solar project can become proof of what is possible and inspire broader adoption throughout the community.

Advocate for Clean Energy

Use your trusted voice to explain the environmental and economic benefits of solar and show how energy savings strengthen your mission.

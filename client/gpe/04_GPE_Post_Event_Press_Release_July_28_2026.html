Atlanta Leaders Call for Continued Investment in Environmental Justice + Climate Resilience

Residents, advocates, elected officials, and environmental leaders gathered at Patagonia Beltline to elevate community-led solutions and defend the protections communities need to remain healthy, safe, and resilient.

ATLANTA, GA — July 28, 2026 — [INSERT ATTENDANCE OR A GENERAL DESCRIPTION OF THE CROWD] gathered Tuesday morning at Patagonia Beltline for Atlanta Communities Demand Protection Against Attacks on Environmental Justice, a public event hosted by Girl Plus Environment.

The event brought together residents and leaders to discuss the environmental challenges Atlanta communities are already navigating, including extreme heat, poor air quality, increasing energy burdens, and disproportionate exposure to environmental hazards. Speakers connected those local realities to federal rollbacks that threaten environmental justice programs and environmental protections.

Diamond Spratling, Executive Director of Girl Plus Environment, opened the event and was joined by Representative Dr. Jasmine Clark of Georgia House District 108; Michael Hawthrone Jr., founder of the Klean Lifestyle Movement; Dr. Jazmine Nicholson, executive director of the Earthgang Foundation; and [INSERT FINAL SPEAKER NAME AND TITLE, OR REMOVE THIS PHRASE].

“Environmental justice is about people. It is about whether families can stay safe during extreme heat. It is about whether children can breathe clean air. It is about whether communities have the resources they need to thrive. We have seen what is possible when communities are supported, and we refuse to move backwards.”

— Diamond Spratling, Executive Director, Girl Plus Environment

About Girl Plus Environment

Girl Plus Environment is a Georgia-based nonprofit committed to educating, engaging, and empowering Black and Brown women and femmes in the climate and environmental justice movements. Through organizing, advocacy, and culturally relevant programming, GPE is building a more inclusive, sustainable future from the ground up.

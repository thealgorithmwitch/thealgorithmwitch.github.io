This Climate Creator Cohort Partnership Agreement (“Agreement”) is entered into by and between Girl Plus Environment (“GPE”) and the creator identified below (“Creator”). GPE and Creator may each be referred to as a “Party” and together as the “Parties.”

02

Purpose and Term

GPE’s Climate Creator Cohort is a three-month paid creator partnership that connects trusted digital creators with climate advocacy campaigns designed to make issues such as extreme heat, rising energy bills, clean energy, and community resilience accessible, culturally relevant, and actionable.

The Agreement begins on the Effective Date and continues until all required deliverables have been completed, all amounts due have been paid, and all surviving obligations have been satisfied, unless terminated earlier under this Agreement.

Creator is not required to present as a climate expert. GPE will provide campaign information and approved resources, while Creator will translate those materials through Creator’s own voice, storytelling style, and relationship with their audience.

03

Services and Deliverables

Creator will create, submit for review, and publish three (3) original short-form vertical videos, consisting of one (1) video per month. Each video should generally be between thirty (30) and ninety (90) seconds unless GPE approves another length in writing.

Creator is responsible for creative development, filming, narration, editing, captioning, and publication. Each deliverable must:

  • be original content created for the campaign;
  • align with the applicable monthly campaign brief;
  • include the assigned call to action and petition access method;
  • comply with FTC disclosure requirements and platform rules;
  • use captions and readable on-screen text whenever platform functionality permits;
  • feature Creator rather than an AI-generated replacement unless GPE approves otherwise in writing.
04

Promotion and Platform Requirements

Instagram Collaboration. For each Instagram Reel published under this Agreement, Creator will invite @girlplusenvironment as an Instagram Collaboration Partner before publication so the Reel may appear on both Creator’s and GPE’s feeds. Creator will keep the collaboration active and will not remove GPE as a collaborator without prior written approval.

Creator will tag @girlplusenvironment in the caption and, where reasonably appropriate, within the content. When publishing on TikTok or another approved platform, Creator will tag GPE wherever platform functionality permits and follow campaign-specific hashtag or attribution guidance.

If a technical issue prevents use of the Instagram Collaboration feature, Creator will notify GPE promptly and follow an alternative written tagging or reposting plan approved by GPE.

05

Required Calls to Action and Petition Access

Each deliverable must include a clear invitation for viewers to participate in the advocacy action assigned in the applicable monthly brief. Creator will make the designated link accessible for at least seven (7) consecutive calendar days after publication through either:

  • the designated petition link in Creator’s social media bio;
  • an approved direct-message automation that sends the link to viewers; or
  • another method approved by GPE in writing.

06

FTC Disclosure, Accuracy, and Legal Compliance

Creator will clearly disclose the paid relationship with GPE in each sponsored deliverable. Acceptable disclosures include “Paid partnership with Girl Plus Environment,” #ad, #sponsored, or another disclosure approved by GPE. The disclosure must be clear and conspicuous and should appear in the content or caption; the Instagram Collaboration feature alone does not replace the disclosure obligation.

Creator will comply with applicable laws, platform rules, advertising standards, and endorsement requirements. Creator will not knowingly make false or misleading statements about GPE, a campaign, a petition, climate impacts, public policy, or another factual matter.

GPE will provide campaign facts and educational materials. Creator may express authentic opinions and personal experiences but will not materially alter supplied factual information in a way that changes its meaning.

07

Creative Process, Review, and Revisions

GPE values Creator’s authentic voice and does not require scripted content. Except for factual accuracy, required campaign elements, legal disclosures, accessibility, and brand identification, Creator retains discretion over format, tone, storytelling, humor, trends, editing, wardrobe, and visual style.

Creator will submit each draft before publication using a private link, watermarked export, shared drive, or another agreed method. GPE will provide approval, consolidated revisions, or questions within three (3) business days. If GPE does not respond within that period, the draft will be considered approved unless the Parties agree otherwise in writing.

One (1) round of reasonable revisions is included for each deliverable. Revision requests will be limited to factual corrections, missing required elements, accessibility, FTC compliance, the required petition or call to action, and material branding inaccuracies. Major changes caused by a new campaign direction after approval require mutual written agreement.

08

Performance Reporting

Within fourteen (14) calendar days after each deliverable is published, Creator will provide reasonably available performance analytics, including views, reach, impressions, likes, comments, shares, saves, profile visits, link clicks, and DM automation activity when applicable.

Analytics may be submitted as screenshots, platform exports, or another mutually agreed format. A platform’s failure to provide a particular metric does not constitute breach.

10

Ownership, License, and Usage Rights

Creator retains copyright ownership of Creator’s original deliverables. Creator grants GPE a perpetual, worldwide, non-exclusive, royalty-free license to repost and display approved deliverables organically across GPE-owned social media, websites, newsletters, presentations, reports, grant materials, educational resources, and organizational archives.

GPE may resize, crop, caption, subtitle, add accessibility features, and add reasonable organizational branding, but will not materially change Creator’s message without written approval. GPE will provide Creator attribution whenever reasonably practical.

This Agreement does not include paid advertising, boosting, whitelisting, dark posting, account access, or use of Creator’s likeness in paid media. Those rights require a separate written agreement.

Neither Party may use the other Party’s content, voice, likeness, image, or proprietary materials to train an artificial-intelligence model without prior written consent.

11

Content Retention

Creator will keep each sponsored deliverable publicly available for at least twelve (12) months after publication and will not delete, archive, make private, or materially edit the deliverable without GPE’s prior written approval.

GPE will reasonably consider exceptions involving platform removal, legal requirements, safety, harassment, threats, account compromise, or another legitimate circumstance.

12

Creator Representations and Warranties

Creator represents and warrants that Creator has authority to enter this Agreement; deliverables will be original or properly licensed; Creator has permission to use included music, images, graphics, footage, locations, and third-party likenesses; and deliverables will not knowingly infringe intellectual-property, privacy, publicity, or other rights.

Creator will not knowingly include defamatory, fraudulent, discriminatory, unlawful, or materially misleading content. Artificial-intelligence-generated presenters, cloned voices, avatars, or synthetic likenesses may not replace Creator unless GPE approves their use in writing.

13

Community Standards and Brand Safety

Both Parties will communicate respectfully and participate in good faith. Either Party may terminate this Agreement if the other engages in unlawful conduct or public conduct reasonably likely to cause material reputational harm, including hate speech, discrimination, threats, violence, fraud, or conduct materially inconsistent with GPE’s mission.

Termination under this section does not eliminate compensation already earned for completed and approved work, except where payment may lawfully be withheld due to fraud, infringement, or material breach.

14

Confidentiality

Creator may receive non-public campaign plans, embargoed announcements, strategy, internal communications, partner information, or unreleased materials. Creator will use confidential information only to perform under this Agreement and will not disclose it without GPE’s written authorization, except as required by law.

Confidentiality does not apply to information that was already lawfully known, becomes public through no breach, is received lawfully from a third party, or is independently developed without use of confidential information. These obligations survive termination.

15

Late Deliverables, Cancellation, Termination, and Force Majeure

Late Deliverables. Creator will notify GPE promptly of an anticipated delay. A deliverable more than five (5) business days late without prior communication or approval may be cancelled, may result in delayed payment, or may constitute material breach.

Cancellation by GPE. If GPE cancels before production begins, no fee is owed for that deliverable. If GPE cancels after Creator has substantially completed production but before approval, GPE will pay fifty percent (50%) of that deliverable’s fee. If GPE cancels after approving the deliverable, the full deliverable fee is owed.

Cancellation by Creator. Creator may withdraw by written notice. Creator retains compensation earned for completed and approved work, while future unpaid deliverables are cancelled.

Termination for Cause. Either Party may terminate immediately for material breach, unlawful conduct, knowing misrepresentation, infringement, or inability to perform. The breaching Party should be given a reasonable opportunity to cure when the breach can be cured.

Force Majeure. Neither Party is liable for delay caused by events beyond reasonable control, including serious illness, family emergency, severe weather, natural disaster, internet or platform outage, government action, war, civil emergency, or comparable event. The affected Party will provide prompt notice and the Parties will work in good faith to revise deadlines.

16

Independent Contractor, Indemnification, and General Terms

Independent Contractor. Creator is an independent contractor and not an employee, partner, agent, or joint venturer of GPE. Creator controls the manner and means of production and is responsible for Creator’s taxes, insurance, equipment, assistants, and business expenses.

Indemnification. Creator will indemnify and hold GPE and its directors, officers, employees, and representatives harmless from third-party claims arising from Creator’s breach, infringement, unauthorized materials, negligence, or unlawful conduct. GPE will indemnify Creator from third-party claims arising solely from campaign materials or factual information supplied directly by GPE and used by Creator without material modification.

Governing Law. This Agreement is governed by Illinois law, without regard to conflict-of-law rules. The Parties will attempt in good faith to resolve disputes informally before formal proceedings. Any legal action will be brought in a court of competent jurisdiction in Cook County, Illinois, unless applicable law requires otherwise.

Entire Agreement. This Agreement and its schedules constitute the complete agreement concerning the partnership and supersede prior discussions. Amendments must be in writing and agreed to by both Parties. If a provision is unenforceable, the remaining provisions remain effective. A waiver on one occasion is not a continuing waiver.

Electronic Signatures. Electronic signatures, electronic records, and counterparts have the same force and effect as originals. The Parties consent to receiving the completed Agreement by email.

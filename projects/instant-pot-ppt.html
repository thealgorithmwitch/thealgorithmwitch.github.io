Slide 03 / What’s Working / What’s Not

Current Strengths

High recognition. Real utility. Search gravity.

  • Brand recognition still gives Instant Pot unusual household legitimacy.
  • Product utility is strong enough to support education, troubleshooting, and meal systems.
  • Pinterest and search behavior already fit the product truth.
  • Creator potential is high because the appliance belongs in realistic homes.

Current Gaps

TikTok underpowered. Instagram too polished. Participation too thin.

  • TikTok feels weak relative to brand size and cultural familiarity.
  • Unrealistic kitchens and scripted influencer reads flatten the lived-in appeal.
  • Weak platform-native sizing and bland product presentation reduce energy.
  • Link-in-bio continuity, UGC, collabs, and comment participation need stronger systems.

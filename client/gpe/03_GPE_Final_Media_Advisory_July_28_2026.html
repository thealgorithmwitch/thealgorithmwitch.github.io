Background

Atlanta communities continue to confront extreme heat, poor air quality, rising energy burdens, and disproportionate exposure to environmental hazards. The gathering will connect those local realities to federal actions that threaten environmental justice programs and environmental protections while elevating the community-led solutions already helping neighborhoods become healthier and more resilient.

This is an outdoor event. Media should arrive prepared for weather conditions and allow time to locate the event space and complete setup.
